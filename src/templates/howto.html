How to use WIMSical

When you register a device from the My Devices page, a unique QR code is generated for your device.


Print it out or set it to be your lockscreen; make sure that if someone picked up your device, they would be able to find and successfully scan the code. Try scanning the code yourself to make sure it works.


When a stranger scans the code, they will be directed to a page where they can enter their contact info to claim the reward.


If you lose a device, check back often to see if someone has claimed your device. If it has been found, you will be able to see their contact info from the Device Info page.
